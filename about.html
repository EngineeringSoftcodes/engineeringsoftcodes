When was Engineering Softcodes™ created?

Engineering Softcodes™ was created in 2023 with a different name, Engineering Maker Studio, but it changed to Engineering Softcodes™. At the time, they were planning which games to create.

What is Engineering Softcodes™'s first game?

The first game was Gimjam, a 2D Action Platformer with family-friendly gameplay, graphics, and more. But it was canceled. Now, the first game is Insane Forest.

Where is Engineering Softcodes™ from?

Engineering Softcodes™ is from Poland but is based between Poland and Germany.

Who created Engineering Softcodes™?

Engineering Softcodes™ was created by a Content Creator, Animator, and Gamer called PolandLet’sPlay in 2023.
